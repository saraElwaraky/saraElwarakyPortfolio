About Me

Hi, I’m Sara Alwaraky a front-end developer who loves turning ideas into interactive, beautiful, and user-friendly websites.
For me, coding isn’t just about writing lines of code; it’s like painting with pixels and bringing designs to life. I work mainly with React, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, but I’m always exploring new tools and technologies to keep my work fresh and exciting. I’ve built everything from e-commerce stores to task management apps, always focusing on making them smooth, responsive, and enjoyable to use. When I’m not deep into code, you’ll probably find me experimenting with new UI designs, reading about the latest web trends, or improving my skills through creative projects. I believe that every website has a story to tell, and I love being the one who helps tell it.
My goal? To create digital experiences that not only work perfectly but also make people smile when they use them.
